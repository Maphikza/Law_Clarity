Welcome to The Legislative Library

A website created for compliance and law professionals. On this site, you'll find actual legislation as well as simplified summaries in plain language for each section. We hope you find this site helpful and easy to use!

We created this website to make it easier for compliance and law professionals to read and understand legislation. We believe that by providing a simplified summary of each section, professionals will be able to more easily and quickly digest the information and communicate it to others. We hope that this website will help make the law more accessible to everyone. On this website, you can click on the legislation button, find the legislation you are looking for, and it will be laid out in a simple and easy-to-understand format.
